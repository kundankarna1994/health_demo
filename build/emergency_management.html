MANAGEMENT
  1. Initial assessment and resuscitation: Maintain airway, breathing, circulation
  2. Establish the most likely cause of dyspnea and initiate treatment.
  3. Acute exacerbation of COPD/ Acute bronchial asthma
    • Bronchodilators
    • Steroids
    • Antibiotics (if presence of infective exacerbation)
  4. Acute pulmonary edema
    • Diuretics
    • Antihypertensives
  5. Pneumonia
    • Antibiotics
  6. Pneumothorax / Pleural effusion
    • Tube thoracostomy
  7. Pulmonary embolism
    • Anticoagulants
